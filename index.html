Northbay Tech Solutions - Candidate Evaluation

Role: Junior Operations Associate

Welcome to the Evaluation Task

You work at Northbay Tech Solutions, a mid-sized Canadian technology firm. The company is currently hiring for a new position within its operations department, and your input has been requested to evaluate candidates for the role.

You will review a job posting followed by the resumes of two candidates. For each candidate, you will be asked to:

  1. Provide your initial assessment of how well the candidate fits the position.
  2. View a recommendation from the company’s decision-support advisor (AI-based system).
  3. Provide your final evaluation after receiving the advisor’s input.

At the end of the task, you will be asked to choose which candidate to invite for an interview.

Job Posting

Position: Junior Operations Associate

Department: Operations

Location: Toronto Office

Employment Type: Full-time

Seniority Level: Entry-Level / Non-Managerial

Description: Northbay Tech Solutions is seeking an energetic and detail-oriented Junior Operations Associate to support internal coordination, logistics, and day-to-day operations. This is an entry-level role that provides a unique opportunity to work cross-functionally with different teams across the organization.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Coordinate internal scheduling and assist with daily workflow management.
  • Maintain and update documentation, logs, and team records.
  • Support operations managers in cross-functional project tracking.
  • Ensure timely communication between departments.

Desired Qualifications:

  • Bachelor’s degree in business, Communications, or a related field.
  • Familiarity with productivity tools (e.g., Google Workspace, Excel).
  • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
  • Attention to detail and problem-solving mindset.

Question: In general, do you think the company has adequately outlined the most important qualifications for the new Junior Operations Associate position listed above? Why?
